My Developer Journey

Hello! My name is Ruslana Habokan. As a passionate beginner developer, I poured my heart into creating Joker's Sparkle Party. My ultimate goal is to bring real value, joy, and a polished experience to every person who downloads my app.

Current Status: 100% Free!
Right now, the app is completely free. There are no paid features, no subscriptions, and no hidden costs. I want you to simply enjoy the game and the effort I've put into it.

Looking to the Future: Game development takes a massive amount of time, learning, and resources. To help support my growth as a developer and to keep improving the app with exciting new content, I plan to introduce premium features and subscriptions in the future to generate income.

Thank you for being part of this sparkling journey and supporting independent development!
